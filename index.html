Biography

I was born in 1989 in Cracow, Poland and I have been living and working there ever since. I graduated Graphics at Academy of Fine Arts in Cracow in 2011 with Bachelor's Degree. Two years later I received Master's Degree in Industrial Design there.

In 2012 I was recognized with the Rector's scholarship for the Top Students. In 2013 I won the Main Prize for the Best Design Diploma in Poland. I also took part in the diploma exhibition in the Art Palace in Cracow and Polish Design Focus at DMY International Design Festival in Berlin. For the last few years I have been successfully working as a designer in IT and other fields.

My artwork stands in opposition to the design process and digital world. I am trying to create untouchable space using language reserved only for paint. My work is a tribute for the act of creation, the idea of the paintings comes from shapes which are gradually appearing on the canvas. I am trying to grasp the story of looseness and complicity of surrounding chaos impossible to be told in any other way.
