Biography

I was born in 1989 in Cracow, Poland. I graduated Graphics at Academy of Fine Arts in Cracow in 2011 with Bachelor Degree. Master Degree in Industrial Design at Academy of Fine Arts in 2013. In 2012 got Rectors scholarship for the Best Students. In 2013 I was rewarded Main Prize for the Best Design Diploma in Poland. Since 2013 successfully working as a Designer among others in the Technology Companies.

The artwork which I am creating is in opposition to design process and computer work. I am trying to create space untouchable in digital world using language reserved only for paints. My work is tribute for act of creation, the idea of the paintings come from shapes which is appearing on the canvas. I am trying to catch looseness and complicity of surrounding chaos impossible to be told in other way.
